About Us

We are a free service

Here to support all students of SCCCD

You read that right the Clovis Community College Tutorial Center is totally free to use by any student registered in any of the SCCCD college campuses, Fresno, Clovis, Reedley, Madera, and Oakhurst. Included in your low monthly payment of $0 you have access to our award winning tutors, unlimited S.T.E.M. drop-in access, up to two 30 minute writing appointments (with a break in between) a day. We also provide:

  • Study Sessions that are focused on a particular section or topic that your instructor feels may need more attention.
  • Comm Lab, to practice any speech you have.
  • Group Tutoring for more help with writing.
  • Write Night, a place to work any piece of writing not just academic.
  • Book Club, a nice place to read and discuss books.
  • Open Mic Night, express yourself through song, poem, or just speak you mind.
  • Power Up Week, the week before finals we have extended hours to help you with that last minute studying.
  • Pre-Covid parties where you could meet the tutors and become familiar with the Tutorial Center, We hope to someday bring these back.

Again all these services are free to all SCCCD students, just come join our Zoom meeting during normal operating hours.
