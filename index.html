About My Dog Kandi

So this is my dog kandi.
She is a Staffordshire Bull Terrier and is just over 10 years old.
When I first got her she was a tiny little thing.

Now she is showing her age. Her once jet black snout has greyed.
And she has torn the ACLs of both of her knees. More recently she has had to have cancerous tumors removed from her sides and neck, but she has remained a happy little piggy and hopefully still has a few more good years left.
