Social Media

Nowadays everyone is on social media, checking their feed, sending tweets into the twitterverse, or updating their Friendster accounts. Here at the Clovis Community College Tutorial Center we have decide to attempt to leverage this new way of communicating that has everyone glued to their phones. You can follow and interact with us on Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram. So come check our status and keep up to date on all our events and any updates to our hours of operation.
