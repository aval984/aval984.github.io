Humanities

Our humanities tutors are here to help with all your writing needs. Everything from an English 1A paper to help with filling out transfer forms to a university. Along with English classes our humanities tutors are able to help in such classes as Psych, ASL, Comm, and Phil. To see a humanities tutor please be aware that an appointment is required, will also offer a Comm Lab to help you write and practice any speeches you may have.
