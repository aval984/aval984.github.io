Zoom

Due to current events the Clovis Community College Tutorial Center has moved to a completely online service for the Fall 2020 and Spring 2021 semesters. Like many colleges and universities we have chosen to use Zoom to conduct our online tutoring services. You can connect with us on Zoom using your PC, Mac, tablet, or even from your smart phone. For the best experience it is recommended to use your webcam and microphone to communicate with our tutors.
