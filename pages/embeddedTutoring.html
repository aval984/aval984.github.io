Embedded Tutoring

Embedded Tutoring is special in the way that a tutor is embedded in a classroom. They attend all class meetings and assist the instructor in various ways. Because the Tutor has already taken the class, they are able to provide a unique viewpoint to the students. Embedded Tutors are not teachers aids and cannot grade student work.
