Drop-in

Our Drop-in service is primarily for S.T.E.M., you can come in any time during our hours of operation and visit with a S.T.E.M. tutor. We try to have a wide range of subjects covered at all times, however it may be the case that a particular tutor is busy with multiple tutees and it may take some time before they can assist you. There is also a Drop-in for our Comm Lab, where you can get help at any stage of a speech, from brainstorming to practicing timing.
